Research Interests
------
My research is centred on natural language processing, motivated by the goal of broadening reliable information access across languages and domains. I am currently focused on:

* **General-purpose retrieval across domains** &nbsp;—&nbsp; building robust systems that can surface trustworthy information across heterogeneous formats and topics for complex real-world queries.
* **Multilingual open-domain QA** &nbsp;—&nbsp; improving information accessibility for speakers of underrepresented languages, with an emphasis on systems that work from very limited supervision.
